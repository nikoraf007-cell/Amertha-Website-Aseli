Company Profile

PT. Amartha Teknik Sejahtera – A company specializing in conveyor belt products and solutions under the ATS brand and the globally recognized Continental brand. Serving industries such as mining, ports, construction, manufacturing, logistics, and agribusiness. Established in 2025, committed to delivering durable and efficient solutions. Supported by professional services including product selection and installation.

Our products are widely used in industries such as mining, coal, metallurgy, ports, power plants, construction, food processing, and transportation for efficient material handling. They have been exported to more than 40 countries across Europe, America, Australia, Asia, and Africa.

Vision

To become Indonesia's most trusted, leading, and innovative provider of conveyor belt products and industrial conveyor system solutions, consistently delivering sustainable added value to customers, partners, and national industries through superior product quality, professional service, and the implementation of modern, efficient, and environmentally friendly technology.

Mission

  • Provide high-quality products complying with national & international standards.
  • Deliver efficient conveyor solutions for mining, manufacturing, logistics, agribusiness.
  • Establish broad distribution network for fast service across Indonesia.
  • Build long-term partnerships based on trust & professionalism.
  • Develop competent human resources committed to safety & customer satisfaction.
